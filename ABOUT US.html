About Muterere Secondary School
       Muterere Secondary School is a community-focused secondary school in Kampala, Uganda. We provide quality education from Senior One to Senior Six, developing both the mind and character of our students.
Our Motto: We Strive to Excel
    This motto reminds us that excellency is a journey.
      Excellence is our daily goal. We encourage hard work, integrity, and continuous improvement in class and beyond.
Our Core Values:
     Excellence: Highest standards in learning and service.
     Discipline: Respect, order, and self-control.
     Integrity: Honest and accountable, always.
     Teamwork: We grow better together.
     Respect: For self, others, and community
 Our Mission
     The mission of Muterere Secondary School is to provide holistic, inclusive, and high-quality education that empowers students academically, morally, and socially. We are committed to creating a disciplined, safe, and supportive learning environment where every student is inspired to discover their talents, develop critical thinking, and build strong character.
     Through dedicated teachers, modern teaching methods, and active participation in academics, science, ICT, sports, and co-curricular activities, we strive to nurture responsible citizens who are prepared to meet the challenges of the 21st century.
       Guided by our motto, "We Strive to Excel," we encourage a culture of hard work, integrity, teamwork, and continuous improvement. We partner with parents, the community, and stakeholders in Kampala District to ensure that every learner at Muterere Secondary School grows in knowledge, leads with confidence, and serves with excellence
